Course Outcomes

  • Write a formal notation for strings, languages and machines.
  • Design finite automata to accept a set of strings of a language.
  • Determine whether the given language is regular or not.
  • Design context free grammars and pushdown automata for context free languages.
  • Design Turing machines for simple problems.
  • Understand the logical limits to computational capacity.
  • Get proper insight on undecidable problems.
